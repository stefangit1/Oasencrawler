# OASENCRAWLER

C++ Console Game  
- Collect Relics and try to dodge the enemy.  
- The Gameboard resets and difficulty raises once you collect all relics.  
- Try not to die by stepping on danger fields.  
- Wells restore 1 health point.  
- Entering a Danger field can trigger one of three minigames  
- Collect items by stepping on relics or wells.  
- Use Items to avoid danger field minigames.
